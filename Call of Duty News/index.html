What is the Meta going to be in the Call of Duty League?

Most of the players think that the MP40 will be the go to SMG, while the STG will be the main AR to run during the tournaments to come.


What will be the main maps played in Vanguard for the Call of Duty League?

The maps that I have seen most being played is BOCAGE, TUSCAN, BERLIN, and CASTLE. That being said, I hope the Call of Duty League brings in GAVUTU, NUMA NUMA, and EAGLE'S NEST.

These maps will be a great add-on to the league play, because of the fast pace play, which will make for a great viewing experience for the fans.




What maps do you want to see them bring back from previous titles?

SHIPMENT, GIBRALTAR, LONDON DOCKS, and ROUND HOUSE (World at War).
